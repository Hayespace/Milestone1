Gaia Dipilato

Artist | Graphic Designer

GA/LAB is my personal creative escape. Creating has always kept me active no matter how busy my daily life. After leaving Naples back in 2013, I wandered through different cities of Europe eventually finding a home in the beautiful Palma de Mallorca where I now live with my beautiful family. Here, I have turned my focus to painting predominantly on textiles and clothing. With portraits my main focus, I enjoy creating and exploring different techniques from watercolours, ink, acrylics, and graphic design.
I am an academic artist and self taught graphic designer with 10 years experience working with and creating custom designs for street apparel and footwear. A lifelong dedicated artist and designer, I love to push the boundaries of my skillset and adopt different styles, techniques and mediums. As a freelancer, my previous commissions have enabled me to design logos, film posters, portraits and textile prints.
